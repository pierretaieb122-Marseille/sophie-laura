The mission

Universities shape the habits of millions of students across Europe every day. We work with institutions to make plant-based food the default, not the exception. Our movement is built on a well‑tested theory of change that tackles the problem at every level:

  • Material change. Millions of meals have already been shifted by Plant-Based Universities. Hundreds of millions more are within reach.
  • Narrative change. What universities serve reshapes social norms for an entire generation, and frankly, for society as a whole.
  • Transformative change. This movement has changed the lives of thousands of students, teaching them that they can make a difference. That is our personal story too.

Who we are

Laura Wenzel
Executive Director

Laura leads the movement's network team: ten people responsible for growing PBU across every front, from communications and fundraising to leadership training, infrastructure and strategic recruitment. The team has doubled in size over the past year and is set to grow a further 40% in the year ahead. Laura also chairs the coordination team, the movement's highest decision‑making body.

Sophie Wenzel
Movement Director

Sophie directs the movement. She oversees eight national coordinators, each responsible for a network of local campaigns spanning more than 70 universities. She steers PBU's international strategy, supports coordinators in the field and leads expansion into new territories. Italy, Poland and Portugal are currently in the exploratory phase. The movement is growing fast, and Sophie is the reason why.
