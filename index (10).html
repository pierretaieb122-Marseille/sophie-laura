The mission

Universities shape hundreds of thousands of meals across Europe every day. We work with institutions to make plant-based food the standard, not the exception. Our movement is built on a well‑tested theory of change that tackles the problem at every level:

  • Material change. Millions of meals have already been shifted by Plant-Based Universities. Hundreds of millions more are within reach.
  • Narrative change. What universities serve reshapes social norms for an entire generation, and frankly, for society as a whole.
  • Transformative change. This movement has changed the lives of thousands of students, teaching them that they can make a difference. That is our personal story too.

Who we are

Laura Wenzel
Executive Director

Laura leads PBU’s international Network Team, a nine-person team supporting the growth of the Plant-Based Universities movement across Europe. She oversees communications, fundraising, leadership training, recruitment, and the organisational infrastructure that provide the conditions for this fast-growing, volunteer-powered movement to run.

Sophie Wenzel
Movement Director

Sophie directs the movement. She trains and oversees 8 National Coordinators, each responsible for a network of local campaigns spanning more than 75 universities. She steers PBU's international strategy, supports coordinators in the field and leads the expansion to new countries.
